About Me

I am a(n aspiring) data scientist. I like everything about data - but my interests mainly involve extracting patterns from data (Data Mining), visualizing and presenting data in a easy to understand format (Data Visualization), analysing and understanding what the data can tell and foretell (Statistical Learning - Machine and Deep Learning).


Formally I have a degree in Physics (Master of Sciences, Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Kolkata, India, 2017) and Statistics (Master of Sciences, Leiden University, 2021) with both my theses mainly involving simulation studies and method development. I am proficient in both Python and R but have also used other languages for course assignments or as per the requirements of the project. Being creative and an engergetic person, I have always earned praises from my supervisors, but what excites me most is the ability to dive into new, innovative and ambitious ideas and see those ideas to fruition.


In my free time I generally read story books or play computer games or learn a new programming language. I also like to travel (my most favourite destination visited recently is Prague). Pre-Corona I was also active in sports specially in Basketball (represented my college in national level tournament for research institutes), have played cricket for several years and learnt volleyball and table-tennis at my previous institute. Apart from that I love swimming.
